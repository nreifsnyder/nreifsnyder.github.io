Lacrosse, The Best Sport

Lacrosse is, by far, my favorite sport.

How you score is as simple as get the ball into your opponents goal. How its played, on the other hand, is quite rough. For example, if a player has the ball, or is about 5 yards form the ball, you can body check them.

    The equipment you need to play:
  • Gloves
  • Helmet
  • Stick
  • Padding (if you want)
  • Cleats

And possibly some more.
